    - `score_total: real('score_total')` — the final 0-100 score (null until first scoring run)
    - `score_risk_adjusted: real('score_risk_adjusted')` — 40% component sub-score (0-100)
    - `score_win_rate: real('score_win_rate')` — 20% component sub-score (0-100)
    - `score_consistency_recency: real('score_consistency_recency')` — 20% component sub-score (0-100)
    - `score_activity_health: real('score_activity_health')` — 20% component sub-score (0-100)
    - `trade_count: integer('trade_count')` — total swap rows at time of scoring
    - `recent_trade_count: integer('recent_trade_count')` — swap rows with timestamp >= (now - 180 days)

    All seven new columns should be nullable (no `.notNull()`) — they are null until the first scoring run writes them.

    Keep all existing columns in wallet_metrics unchanged. Do NOT change any other tables.
